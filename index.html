Those goals are obtaining a GPA of at least 3.5, join many medical related clubs,volunteer my time at local or non-local hospitals or clinics to learn more about this profession, and study hard for the MCAT to score well on it. When I am not doing school-related activities, I have other hobbies that I like to partake in. They include playing basketball, lifting weights, spending time with friends and familiy, and going to parties/concerts occasionally.

The reason why the medical field first of all interests me is because I find the human mind and body very fascinating and along with that, I love to help people with their probelems, so with the extensive knowledge of how the brain and body works, I can hopefully help many people.

The reason why Psychiatry and Neurology interests me the most, compared to the rest of the medical subjects that I have studied so far, is the fact that the brain, something that we all as human beings have, can create such variation between people, meaning that because of our own brain, we are all individual people who act, think, and behave like no one else in the world. This also leads to problems because the way one has to treat a mental disability is unique because of that notion. Since we are all different mentally and emotionally, we must approach mental and emotional problems in a different manner than physical ones, and that is something I look forward to doing in the future.
